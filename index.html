What is Momodora: Reverie Under the Moonlight

Momodora: Reverie Under the Moonlight is a 2D Metroidvania Sidescroller published by Playism and developed by Brazillian studio Bombservice. The game focuses on a priestess named Kaho on a quest to remove a curse corrupting the land. In every area Kaho must defeat a boss while also collecting the four pieces of a crest required to access the castle and face the Queen.

Overview of the User Interface

In this report I heuristically analyse the interface of Momodora: Reverie Under the Moonlight, as the game is developed by a small indie company it is interesting to focus on a product produced on a lower budget than comparative AAA titles. The interface of Momodora: Reverie Under the Moonlight is very simplistic, consisting of a scrolling camera and three interface elements: A box showing the current equipped item, a health bar, and a counter for currency used to buy items.
